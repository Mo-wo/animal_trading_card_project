Norweigian Forest Cat

They appear in Norse mythology, as the cats that pulled the chariot of Freya, goddess of love and beauty.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Common Nicknames: Skogatt
  • Height: 9-12 inches
  • Weight: 12-22 pounds
  • Life span: 14-16 years
  • Coat length: Long
  • Intelligence: High

Their water-resistant coat with a dense undercoat developed to help the cat survive in the harsh Scandinavian climate.
